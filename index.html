THE HIDDEN HARMONY

the synesthetic relationship
between colours and music

the·background
In 2017 I was attending my senior year
of high school and I was looking for a
project that I cold submit for my graduation exam. One morning, traveling from home to the school, I was mesmerized by the autumn colours of the fields and sky. Then I understood what would be my project based on.

The relationship between colours and music pitch have more than one theory.
From Greeks with Aristotle: On Colours,
to Isaac Newton: Opticks and many other
artists like Kandinsky: On the
Spiritual in Art , and Skrjabin in
Prometheus: The Poem of Fire .
For this reason the type of relationship that I will use, it’s more
a theoretical view, rather than an empirical evidence.
the·idea
It is pretty simple: create a method that could be used for realizing
various creative systems and installations.

Starting from an image, the process
has to give in response a set of notes
starting from a set of colours.
How·IT·WORKS
The method I designed describes stable
relation, by considering two main relations between colours and sound frequencies:

the luminance with octaves and the hue value with the notes of a single octave.

IMPLEMENTATIONS
This type of work can be the backbone for many many other projects and installations,
considering not only the translation from colour to sound, but also the reverse process,
that can be used as basis for A/V installations
and also real-time executions.
